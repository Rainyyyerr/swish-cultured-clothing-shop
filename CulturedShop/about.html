WELCOME TO THE CLUB.

Founded in Davao City in 2024, SWISH started as a passion project inspired by the local underground band scene. Driven by the founder's love for graphic design, the brand quickly grew into a canvas for bold illustrations and creative exploration. Over time, SWISH gained a dedicated following among bands, artists, and athletes, evolving into one of the Philippines' most recognized yet versatile local brands. Today, SWISH stands as a testament to fearless creativity—constantly adapting, always in pursuit of the new.
